Project : Sistem Pernapasan

Client : Erik | Year : 2020

Topic : This project began in 2020 when a client of FTI UKSW students wanted to make a thesis on the effect of applications on computer-based learning. Starting in early 2020, technology is growing and not spared from elementary school education. This also affects the technology used including the use of laptops between classes, therefore making applications is used to assess the process of each student.

User & Audience : Primary/Middle School Children Grade 5

Requirement :

  • Laptop
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Adobe Flash
  • Corel Draw

Progress :

  • Determine the material (Ministry of Education Material Book for Elementary/Middle School Children)
  • Brainstorming the initial design
  • Designing Digital Designs
  • Designing a Program
  • Export to .exe so it can be run on a laptop
