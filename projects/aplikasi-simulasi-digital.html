Project : Simulasi Digital

Client : Steven Ade | Year : 2020

Topic : This application was started to complete the thesis Designing Interactive Teaching Materials based on Android. In conducting observations, class X students at SMK Negeri 3 Salatiga still use paper and presentation media to undergo learning. In the practicum of video presentation subjects, students use paper media as directions, and presentation media to show the results that have been made. Therefore, a problem was formulated about how to design and build interactive teaching materials based on Android, as well as their suitability in learning on Video Presentation material.

User & Audience : Students in Class X of SMK Negeri 3 Salatiga

Requirement :

  • Laptop
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Adobe Flash
  • Corel Draw
