Project : Magic in My Tiny Heart

Client : Self-Project | Year : 2022

Topic : This video aims to create stop-motion

User & Audience : Public

Requirement :

  • Laptop
  • Vegas Pro 11
  • Papercraft

Progress :

  1. Before making a video, a storyboard is determined to determine the length and tools to be used.
  2. After that, determine the theme for the stop motion short video
  3. The design is then molded and printed so that the papercraft can be made
  4. Then observe suitable places for filming
  5. Due to the rainy season, filming was done in 3 days
  6. Editing is done for 1 day and published on YouTube
